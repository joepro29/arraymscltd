Array Medical Suppliers and Consults Limited

ARRAY MSC LTD is one of the leading biomedical engineering companies in the central region of Uganda. As a biomedical engineering services company, we place great emphasis on the relationship with clients, private and public medical facilities, and pride ourselves on our ability to respond to your medical supplies and hospital equipment requirements in an efficient and cost-effective way.

We undertake biomedical engineering works including installation, service, repair, and maintenance. We are suppliers of quality and reliable medical consumables.

We employ a number of skilled biomedical engineers and technicians, specializing in a vast area including ICU equipment, Laboratory equipment, Hospital design and construction, Technical consultations, Imaging modality experts, Health and safety, Medical waste management, Risk management consultations, and End-user training.
